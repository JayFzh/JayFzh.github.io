About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Bo Jiang. My research interests lie in cloud networking, distributed systems, and AI infrastructure. Recently, I have been focusing on multipath reliable transport protocols and efficient distributed training of dynamic graph neural networks (DGNNs). I am also a research intern at Alibaba Cloud, working on large-scale network system.
